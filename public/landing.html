How it works

Three steps to publication-grade data.

Every data point has a clear owner at each stage. No black boxes, no lost context.

Step 01

Upload & proofread

Import any PDF. AI parses and pre-fills all structured fields, anchored to the source paragraph. Annotators then verify each value in a split-pane view — field by field, with full context visible throughout.

Step 02

Review & resolve

Reviewers see all agent sources — AI, annotator, and prior versions — side by side. Conflict fields are flagged automatically. One click to adopt the best value and lock the decision with a full audit trail.

Step 03

Accept & ingest

Acceptors do a final review per record and push to the database in one click. Re-ingestion overwrites cleanly. Every version is snapshotted, every value traceable back to its origin document.
